about us.

WaitInk is a full service company for ordering, selling and managing business. Fully integrated with today's mobile market.

Our company will specialize in streamlining the ordering and paying options for “horeca” business. We offer the opportunity to browse the menu, order items and pay for these with the consumers own mobile device. Our app will be integrated in our own point of sale system which main parts will be hosted in the cloud available by specialized apps.
